## General Questions

### Q: What is the AI Interview Trainer Agent?

**A:** The AI Interview Trainer Agent is an intelligent conversational system that helps students and professionals prepare for interviews. It uses advanced AI to simulate realistic interview scenarios and provides personalized feedback on your performance.

### Q: How does it work?

**A:** The system works in these steps:
1. You select interview type and difficulty
2. AI generates a relevant question
3. You provide your answer
4. AI evaluates your answer
5. You receive score and feedback
6. Process repeats for more questions

### Q: Who can use this tool?

**A:** Anyone preparing for interviews:
- College students preparing for campus placements
- Professionals seeking new jobs
- Career changers wanting to brush up interview skills
- Anyone wanting to improve communication skills

### Q: Is this a real job interview substitute?

**A:** No, this is a practice and preparation tool:
- ✓ Good for learning and practicing
- ✓ Helps build confidence
- ✓ Identifies weak areas
- ✗ Not a replacement for real interviews
- ✗ But excellent preparation before real interviews

### Q: What topics does it cover?

**A:** Primarily ECE topics:
- Digital Electronics
- Communication Systems
- Microprocessors
- Embedded Systems
- Programming & Data Structures
- General HR & soft skills

### Q: Can I prepare for non-technical interviews?

**A:** Yes! The tool covers:
- HR/Behavioral interviews
- Communication skills
- Personality assessment
- Problem-solving approaches
- General aptitude

## Scoring & Feedback

### Q: How is my score calculated?

**A:** Score depends on question type:

**Technical (0-100):**
- Technical Accuracy: 40%
- Completeness: 30%
- Clarity: 20%
- Examples/Details: 10%

**HR (0-100):**
- Relevance: 30%
- Clarity: 25%
- Depth: 25%
- Professionalism: 20%

### Q: What's a good score?

**A:** Score interpretation:
- **90-100:** Excellent - Ready to interview
- **80-89:** Very Good - Minor improvements needed

### Q: How is feedback generated?

### Q: Is the scoring fair?

### Q: How can I improve my score?

## Technical Issues

### Q: The application won't load

**A:** Try these solutions in order:
1. Check internet connection
2. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. Try different browser
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Restart computer
6. Contact support if persists

### Q: Questions don't appear

**A:** Solutions:
1. Refresh page (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Check internet speed (need 5+ Mbps)
3. Wait 10-15 seconds for loading
4. Try "Skip" and continue
5. End session and start fresh

### Q: My answer won't submit

**A:** Troubleshooting:
1. Ensure text is entered in answer box
2. Check text length (5-5000 characters)
3. Remove special symbols if any
4. Try different browser
5. Clear cookies and retry

### Q: Response is very slow

**A:** Performance optimization:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Check internet speed
5. Use 4G/5G instead of WiFi (if slow)
6. Try during off-peak hours

### Q: Getting error messages

**A:** Common errors:
- "Connection Error" - Check internet
- "Timeout" - Server slow, try again
- "Invalid Session" - Log out and log back in
- "Database Error" - Try again later
- "Rate Limited" - Wait 15 mins, try again

### Q: Application crashes during interview

**A:**
1. Don't worry, session is saved
2. Refresh page to resume
3. Your answers are preserved
4. Continue from where you left off
5. Contact support if lost progress

### Q: Can't log in to account

**A:** Solutions:
1. Verify email address (no extra spaces)
2. Check password (case-sensitive)
3. Reset password if forgotten
4. Check email verification status
5. Clear cookies and try again
6. Contact support

## Tips & Best Practices

### Q: How often should I practice?

**A:** Recommended schedule:
- **Casual learning:** 1-2 sessions/week
- **Active preparation:** 3-4 sessions/week
- **Intensive prep:** Daily (2 weeks before interview)
- **Maintenance:** 1 session/week after getting job

### Q: How do I improve faster?

**A:** Tips for faster improvement:
1. Focus on weak domains
2. Practice 30 mins daily
3. Study ideal answers carefully
4. Take notes during feedback
5. Retake difficult questions
6. Review mistakes
7. Track progress metrics

### Q: What's the best interview type to start with?

**A:** Recommendation:
1. **Week 1:** HR Interview (confidence building)
2. **Week 2:** Technical (easier difficulty)
3. **Week 3:** Technical (harder difficulty)
4. **Week 4:** Campus Placement (realistic simulation)

### Q: Should I study before practicing?

**A:** Yes! Recommended approach:
1. Learn concepts (2-3 days)
2. Practice interviews (1 week)
3. Review weak areas (3-4 days)
4. Practice again (1 week)
5. Take final mock (1-2 days before interview)

### Q: How much improvement can I expect?

**A:** Typical improvement (4 weeks):
- Week 1: 45% (learning phase)
- Week 2: 60% (building confidence)
- Week 3: 72% (significant improvement)
- Week 4: 80%+ (ready for real interview)

### Q: Should I memorize answers?

**A:** Not recommended!
- Memorized answers sound robotic
- Interviewer can tell
- AI penalizes memorized content
- Instead: Understand concepts
- Practice articulation
